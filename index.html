Why We Exist

Our Mission

Communication is a basic human right — yet millions of deaf and speech-impaired people are cut off from everyday conversation, and countless patients struggle through slow, discouraging rehabilitation. We build affordable, intelligent devices that change that.

Mission

To bridge the communication gap for the deaf and speech-impaired community with real-time, AI-powered sign language translation — and make smart rehabilitation accessible to every patient who needs it.

Vision

A world where accessibility is the standard, not an afterthought — where every hand has a voice and every recovery journey is engaging, measurable and dignified.

Impact, Today

We don't stop at prototypes. Gestalk is in daily use at the Government Deaf School, Jagathy, and NeuroPress is helping patients recover at a physiotherapy studio in Trivandrum.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Gestalk is an AI-powered smart electronic glove that captures hand gestures and translates Indian Sign Language into voice, text and images in real time through its companion mobile app. It also converts spoken replies back into text — enabling true two-way conversation between signing and non-signing people.

Gestalk is built for Indian Sign Language (ISL), and its gesture vocabulary can be expanded over time to cover more signs and phrases.

The glove pairs with the Gestalk companion mobile app over Bluetooth. Gestures captured by the glove appear in the app instantly as voice, text and images.

Gestalk runs on a rechargeable battery designed to last through a full day of typical classroom or workplace use, and recharges easily overnight.

Deaf and speech-impaired individuals, special schools, therapy and training centres, and workplaces that want to communicate naturally with signing colleagues, students or customers.

Yes — Gestalk is past the prototype stage. The first units were delivered to the Government Deaf School, Jagathy, Trivandrum in March 2026. For a demo, pricing or an order, get in touch or message us directly on WhatsApp.

Healthcare Innovation

NeuroPress — Squeeze. Play. Recover.

NeuroPress is a smart neuro-rehabilitation device that turns therapy into an engaging game. By combining a pressure-sensing squeeze ball with interactive gameplay, it helps patients rebuild grip strength, motor control and coordination — while making daily exercise genuinely fun.

  • Gamified rehabilitation therapy
  • Grip strength & motor recovery
  • Stress relief & daily fitness
